Blockstream develops products and networks that make financial markets more efficient by decreasing reliance on trust. From enterprise-grade settlement networks, to consumer wallets, to Bitcoin satellites, the company's technology is accelerating the adoption of bitcoin-based peer-to-peer finance. Blockstream's key innovation is in side-chains, which allow the introduction of brand new features to Bitcoin, as well as the issuance of new digital assets.

Blockstream was founded on 2014, with offices and team members distributed around the world.
