Scan connects the real world with the digital universe through mobile scanning technologies such as QR code, barcode, beacon, and other technologies. Scan brings social media, lead generation, and mobile commerce to life through real world "like", "follow", and even "buy" buttons. Companies can easily use Scan's tools to create these custom experiences for their customers. Free at http://scan.me
