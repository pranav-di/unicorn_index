Xendit provides payments infrastructure in South East Asia. We have an end to end solution that allows modern businesses to accept payments through cards and bank transfers, manage funds through escrow/custodian accounts and disburse funds through the fastest pipes in the markets we serve. This means we deliver frictionless payments across tech and traditional businesses.
