Sprig is a mobile application that enables users to find and order healthy meals and have them delivered in a minimal time frame. All the ingredients used in these meals are locally-sourced, sustainable, and seasonal.

Sprig was launched by Morgan Springer, Neeraj Berry, Matt Kent, and Gagan Biyani in April 2013 and is based in San Francisco, United States.
