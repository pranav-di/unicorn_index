Tinder anonymously finds people nearby that like each other and connects them if they are both interested.

Every time you open Tinder, youll immediately see new, interesting people nearby, along with friends or interests you have in common. Swipe right to like or left to pass on each persons profile. All swipes are anonymous. No one will ever know youve passed on their profile. Only once youve both swiped right on each other will your newfound match be revealed.
